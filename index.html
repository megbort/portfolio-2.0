About Me

Megan Krenbrink is a recent graduate of the New Media Design and Web Development program at BCIT, obtaining her diploma in Summer, 2022. She is now is working at Maximizer CRM Software as a Junior Front-End Developer.

Megan grew up on the West Coast of Canada in Victoria, British Columbia. In 2013, she decided to move to Vancouver, British Columbia to attend the Financial Management Program at BCIT. She graduated in 2015 and received a Diploma in Technology. She then worked for 5 years at a digital solutions company before returning to school in 2021.

The experience from both programs has allowed Megan to develop exceptional skills in problem-solving, organization, and communication. She also is proficient in coding languages and tools such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Typescript, Angular, React and more.

Megan has a passion for music and has been playing drums for 18 years. In her spare time likes to play and record music with her band Joey Chaos and the Ghosts. Also in her free time, Megan enjoys drawing, reading, and playing video games.
