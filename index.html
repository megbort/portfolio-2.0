About Me

Megan Krenbrink is a Front-End Developer currently working at Maximizer CRM Software, in Vancouver British Columbia. She previously graduated from the New Media Design and Web Development program at BCIT in 2022.

Megan grew up on the West Coast of Canada in Victoria, British Columbia. In 2013, she decided to move to Vancouver to attend the Financial Management Program at BCIT. She graduated in 2015 and then worked for 5 years at a digital solutions company before returning to school in 2021.

The experience from both programs has allowed Megan to develop exceptional skills in problem-solving, organization, and communication. She also is proficient in coding tools and languages such as Javascript, Typescript, CSS, HTML, NodeJS, Angular and React. She also is actively learning new languages like C# and Python.

Megan has a passion for music and has been playing drums for almost 20 years. In her spare time likes to play and record music with her band Joey Chaos and the Ghosts. Also in her free time, Megan enjoys drawing, reading, and playing video games.
