About Me

Megan Krenbrink is a Software Engineer at Maximizer CRM Software in Vancouver, British Columbia. She started at Maximizer as a Junior Front-End Developer and was promoted to Software Engineer in February 2025.

Megan grew up on the West Coast of Canada in Victoria, British Columbia. In 2013, she decided to move to Vancouver to attend the Financial Management Program at BCIT. She graduated in 2015 and then worked for 5 years at a digital solutions company before returning to school in 2021.

In 2022, Megan graduated from the New Media Design and Web Development program at BCIT, where she gained extensive experience in web development and design.

The experience from both her previous career and her education has allowed Megan to develop exceptional skills in problem-solving, organization, and communication. She is also proficient in coding tools and languages such as JavaScript, TypeScript, CSS, HTML, NodeJS, Angular, React and Vue. Additionally, she is actively learning new languages like C# and Go.

Megan has a passion for music and has been playing drums for over 20 years. In her spare time, she likes to play and record music with her band Joey Chaos and the Ghosts. Also in her free time, Megan enjoys gardening, reading, and playing video games.
