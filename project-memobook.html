Welcome to MemoBook — a multimedia address book where you can keep your contacts and all the memories that come with them! It's like an address book with a twist—you can add photos, timeline events, and more to bring your connections to life.

Built with Vue.js, MemoBook is all about making it fun and easy to keep track of your favorite people and moments. While it's still a work in progress, feel free to explore the concept and see what it's all about!

Enjoy checking out MemoBook!
